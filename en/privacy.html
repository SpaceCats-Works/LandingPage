Last Updated: June 15, 2026

SpaceCatsWorks ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications, PC software, XR applications, and an official website (collectively the "Service"). We are committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how we use it, and your choices regarding it.

2. Types of Information We Collect and Purpose

We handle information in the following contexts:

  • Contact Form: We collect names, email addresses, and message contents submitted through our contact form. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not used for any other purpose.
  • Applications (iOS / Android / PC / XR): Apps released by SpaceCatsWorks do not directly collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information (such as name, address, or email) from users.
  • Website: We may use services such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous access logs for site improvement purposes.

3. Advertising and Analytics (Third-Party SDKs)

The Service may integrate third-party SDKs and services for analytics and advertising purposes. These may collect anonymous identifiers (such as advertising IDs or cookies), device information, and usage telemetry. No personally identifiable information is collected or exposed through these SDKs.

Examples of third-party services used (varies by app/service):

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics (usage analysis)
  • Unity Ads / Unity Analytics (advertising and analysis)
  • Meta Audience Network (advertising)

Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for details on their data practices.

4. Platform-Specific Data Handling

4-1. Apple App Store (iOS / macOS / visionOS)

Apps published on Apple platforms comply with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines and the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

  • If we use the IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) for advertising purposes, we will present an ATT prompt to request tracking permission from the user.
  • If the user declines tracking, we will not perform personalized ad targeting using their identifier.
  • We accurately declare all data types collected and used by each app in the App Store's Privacy Nutrition Labels.
  • For visionOS (Apple Vision Pro) apps, spatial sensor data (such as hand tracking and eye tracking) is processed locally by Apple's platform and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • If an app requires access to HealthKit, location, microphone, camera, or other sensitive resources, the purpose will be clearly stated within the app prior to requesting permission.

4-2. Google Play Store (Android)

Android apps comply with Google's Data Safety requirements and the Google Play Developer Policy.

  • We accurately disclose data types collected, their purposes, and any third-party sharing in Google Play's Data Safety form for each app.
  • If advertising IDs are used, users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings (Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID or opt out).
  • Sensitive permissions (location, camera, microphone, etc.) are requested only when required for a specific feature, and the purpose is explained within the app.
  • Apps targeting families or children comply with Google Play's Families Policy and applicable child privacy regulations.

4-3. Steam / PC (Windows · macOS · Linux)

PC games and software comply with Valve's Steam store policies and distribution requirements.

  • When using the Steamworks SDK, Steam IDs are used for platform features such as achievements and cloud saves. These are managed by Valve and are not collected by us as personal information.
  • Anonymous crash reports or usage telemetry may be collected to improve the product; these are processed in a non-identifiable format.
  • The URL of this Privacy Policy is listed in the Privacy Policy field on each Steam store page.

4-4. Meta Quest (VR / XR)

Meta Quest apps comply with Meta's Developer Policies and Oculus Platform policies.

  • Spatial sensor data generated by room-scale mapping, hand tracking, eye tracking, and similar features is processed locally on-device by Meta's platform. Raw sensor data is not transmitted to our servers.
  • If Meta Audience Network is used for advertising, Meta's data processing terms apply.
  • App privacy practices are disclosed accurately in the Meta Quest Developer Hub data use checklist for each submitted app.
  • Social features (multiplayer, friend lists, etc.) operate through Meta's platform services. We do not manage personally identifiable user data related to social functionality.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies provided by Google Analytics for access analysis. These cookies collect anonymous information such as browser session data and referral URLs to help us improve the Service. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the Service may not function correctly as a result.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, alteration, and leakage of personal information.

If we become aware that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians with concerns may contact us through the Contact Page.
